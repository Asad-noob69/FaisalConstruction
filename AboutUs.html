Who we are.

Our Legacy: Building Excellence since 1999

Faisal Construction Co. has been a pioneering force in the construction industry in Pakistan for over two decades. Our journey began in 1999 with a prestigious enlisted contract from Pakistan International Airlines (PIA), marking the start of our commitment to delivering exceptional building solutions.

A Trailblazer in International Projects

Our expertise soon took us beyond borders, as we ventured into the Middle East with a remarkable project in Muscat, Oman. We constructed numerous villas, showcasing our capability to adapt to diverse environments and exceed client expectations.

Transforming Pakistan's Landscape

Back in Pakistan, we continued to push boundaries with landmark projects in prominent locations:

  • Bahria Town: We built stunning villas, each spanning 500 yards with basements, ground floors, and first floors, redefining luxury living.
  • Golf City, Precinct 6, and Ali Blocks: Our team crafted magnificent structures, setting new standards in residential construction.
  • Maliecantt: We erected a ground-plus-nine-story building, demonstrating our proficiency in high-rise development.

Renovation Mastery

Our expertise extends to renovation work, with an impressive portfolio that includes collaborations with Soneri Bank. We take pride in delivering unique, expertly crafted solutions that breathe new life into existing structures.

A Legacy of Excellence

Throughout our journey, Faisal Construction Co. has remained steadfast in our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. As we continue to build on our legacy, we look forward to shaping the future of Pakistan's construction landscape.
